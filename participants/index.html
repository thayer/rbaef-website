Participants & Sponsors

Participants

The project team brings together leading experts in the diverse fields appropriate to the analysis, as well as institutions representing a broad range of constituents. Participating organizations include:

  • Dartmouth College
  • Natural Resources Defense Council
  • Argonne National Laboratory
  • Michigan State University
  • National Renewable Energy Laboratory
  • Oak Ridge National Laboratory
  • Princeton University
  • Union of Concerned Scientists
  • USDA Agricultural Research Service

Sponsors

Technical and economic analysis is supported by the Department of Energy. Environmental assessment and policy analysis are supported by the Energy Foundation and the National Commission on Energy Policy.
