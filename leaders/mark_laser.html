Task Leaders: Mark Laser

Mark Laser—Co-Leader, Task 4a: Co-product Analysis

Mark Laser is a research associate at Thayer School of Engineering, Dartmouth College. He recently co-authored a report on biomass refining, entitled Strategic Biorefinery Analysis, funded by the National Renewable Energy Laboratory. He also has experience in biomass pretreatment research, as well as bioethanol process design and economic analysis. He earned a BS in chemical engineering from Cornell University and a Ph.D. in engineering sciences from Dartmouth College.
